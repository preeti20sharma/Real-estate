ATS Homekraft Floral Pathways

About Us
ATS Homekraft Floral Pathways is a thoughtfully designed residential development sprawled over approximately 5.3–5.35 acres in Ghaziabad’s NH‑24 corridor. Conceived as a premium project, it comprises 12 towers of up to G+33/G+34 floors, offering around 788 spacious 3 BHK + Study apartments of about 1,720 sq ft. Each floor features just 2 apartments, ensuring low density and abundant privacy. A signature element is the floral-themed landscaped pathways that weave through the project, enhancing the serene ambiance. With nearly 70% open green space, residents enjoy natural light, ventilation, and uninterrupted views, along with a vantage of central greenery from every apartment.
Project amenities include a world-class clubhouse, swimming pool, indoor and outdoor sports facilities, yoga/meditation zones, landscaped gardens, and a kids’ play area. Built with high-quality finishes—anti-skid ceramic flooring, vitrified tiles, granite kitchen counters, branded fixtures—and modern construction methods like Mivan formwork, it delivers both aesthetics and structural integrity. Strategically situated, the project provides seamless connectivity to Noida, Greater Noida West, Anand Vihar, and Delhi, while being in close proximity to schools, hospitals, malls, and marketplaces.
